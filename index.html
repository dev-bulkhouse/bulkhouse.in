Our Business

Bulkhouse is a privately held company incorporated in March 2015 in Visakhapatnam India. Bulkhouse will be operating on a B2B trade model for its International and domestic trading business from its online web portals. A blend of a Marketplace cum store model makes it unique for the Buyers and Vendors to do business on Bulkhouse.

Why Sell on Bulkhouse?

Reach millions through Bulkhouse!
